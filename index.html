SHIPCON SHIPPING PROFILE

Shipcon Shipping & Logistics is a full-service freight forwarding and logistics company founded in 2010, located in Karachi (Pakistan)specializing in import, exportglobal freight and logistics services including: Ocean, Land Freight and customs brokerage services.

At Shipcon Shipping & Logistics, we believe in establishing strong partnerships and close working relationships with our customers to produce a comprehensive total Freight & Logistics Solutions. We continuously provide cost-effective freight, Transportation and Logistics solutions to our broad and varied customer base.
