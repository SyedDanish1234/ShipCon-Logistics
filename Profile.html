SHIPCON LOGISTICS PROFILE

ShipCon Logistics is a full-service freight forwarding and logistics company founded in 2019, located in Karachi (Pakistan)specializing in import, exportglobal freight and logistics services including: Ocean, Air, Land and Rail Freightand customs brokerage services.

At ShipCon Logistics, we believe in establishing strong partnerships and close working relationships with our customers to produce a comprehensive total Freight & Logistics Solutions. We continuously provide cost-effective freight, Ttransportation and Llogistics solutions to our broad and varied customer base.

ShipCon Logistics is able to deal with every type of transportation job, beginning with door-to-door delivery including custom clearance and special goods handling for bulky, dangerous or perishable goods.

ShipCon Logistics also offers logistics services into and out of “hard to reach” and/or “hard to ship”, including destinations with complex import and/or export requirements. With a network of reliable partners, ShipPlus Logistics offers full-service customized freight solutions to clients worldwide.

Having a team of experienced experts onboard, we have been serving many valuable clients with our value added services. Our integrated process combined with seamless coordination enables us to achieve operational efficiency in shipping standard, precious, perishable.

Customer service is our first priority, led by our competitive rates and operational excellency. We believe in our clients as partners; their success is our success. Constant attention to expediting your shipment is just part of the routine at our offices.
